How do I reset my password?

This can be done by either navigating to your profile page and click on the request password button, alternatively you can navigate to the sign in page and click the reset password button there.

How do I create a Debate?

To begin the debate process click the Create Debate button on the navigation bar and follow the process, if you get stuck click on the Modals on the page, else go to the guidance page and watch the video there.

How do I make changes to my profile?

Click on your username in the navigation bar, from there you can make relevant changes and click the "changes" button to confirm the changes made.

How do I contact the admin team about an issue?

Click on the contact information in the Side Panel to there you can fill out a form that will be seen by the admin team.

How do I report a user?

Under each argument is a report button, please click on it and you will be navigated to the reports page, from there fill out the form provided.

Are there any further plans for development?

Keep an eye out on the Future Plans and Latest Announcement page.
