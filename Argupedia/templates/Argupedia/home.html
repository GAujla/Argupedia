Argupedia is a concept proposed by Senior Lecturer and Reader in Artificial Intelligence Sanjay Modgil. In Modern day we often see hundreds of Millions of users take to online platforms to discuss and debate regarding contemporary topics and issues, however the issue is there is no formal structure and no formal evaluation of what arguments are currently winning the debate. This can cause harmful issues such as users not reading the full context of debates and deriving false conclusions. The idea of Argupedia is that users will be able to contribute to an ever-growing repository of debate and opinion, where the data entry has to be structured so as to conform to templates that capture various forms of argument and counter-argument. The arguments are then evaluated using Dungs Grounded semantics and Camindas labelling algorithm .
